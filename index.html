About Me

Hi there! I'm Rodney Lorico Delacruz, a 15-year-old high school student from the beautiful Philippines. My birthday falls on October 13, 2007, which makes me a proud Libra. I'm currently in my 3rd year of high school, and I'm passionate about learning and exploring new ideas.

Family is everything to me. I come from a loving home where my parents, Bonifacia O. Lorico and Ricarido S. Delacruz, have always been my biggest supporters and sources of inspiration. They have instilled in me the values of hard work, perseverance, and kindness, which guide me in everything I do.

Living in the Philippines has given me a unique perspective on life. Our culture is rich and diverse, and I take great pride in embracing the traditions and customs that make our country so special. The beauty of our natural landscapes, the warmth of our people, and the delicious cuisine are just a few things that make the Philippines a truly remarkable place to call home.

In my free time, I have two favorite hobbies that I'm incredibly passionate about. First, I love exploring the vast world of the internet and keeping up with the latest technologies. The internet opens up endless possibilities for learning and connecting with people from all walks of life. Whether it's delving into new subjects, staying updated on global events, or simply finding intriguing content, the internet is an endless source of fascination for me.

My other beloved hobby revolves around PC games. I'm an avid gamer, and I find joy in immersing myself in virtual worlds, exploring exciting realms, and tackling challenging quests. PC gaming not only entertains me but also helps me develop problem-solving skills and teamwork, as many games require cooperation with others.

One of my dreams is to make a positive impact on the world. I believe that education is a powerful tool for transformation, and I aspire to contribute to my community by promoting access to quality education for everyone, regardless of their background.
